About us

At Food Waste Management, we are committed to combating food waste and promoting sustainability. Our vision is to reduce food waste globally by redistributing excess food and raising awareness about the environmental impacts of food waste.



About usThe basic concept of this project Food Waste Management is to collect the excess/leftover food from donors such as hotels, restaurants, marriage halls , etc and distribute to the needy people .
